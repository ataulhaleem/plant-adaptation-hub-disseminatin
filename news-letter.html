Driving Innovation for a Sustainable Future

The Plant Adaptation Hub is more than a database; it is a collaborative ecosystem designed to accelerate breeding cycles, promote data sharing under FAIR principles, and contribute to a more resilient and sustainable agricultural landscape in the EU and beyond.
